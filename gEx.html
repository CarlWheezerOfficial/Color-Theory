Good Examples of Color Theory in Web Design.

Wikipedia

Wikipedia has a simple and neutral color choice made with the specific intent of giving the veiwer a neutral opionion, Wikipedia uses this color pallete as a way to not add any biases to the veiwer as they read as Wikipedia is an unbiased source.

Half-Life.com

The official Half-Life website is simple enough with its color pallate. Generally desaturated greys and whites, heavily contrasted by a vibrant orange that is used throughout the franchise.
