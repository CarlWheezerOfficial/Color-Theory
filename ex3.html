Color theory is used everywhere in design.

From websites to branding to painting, color theory is used to draw the veiwers eye and impact their mind.

Depending on multiple factors such as hue, saturation, and warmth, a design can have a new emotion tied to it, or a new focus.

Color theory is often used in games and artwork to draw the users eye.

One of my absolute favorite examples is the Dishonored franchise. These games have a very painterly style and use color very effectively in leading the player.

This screensshot is from one of the later levels in Dishonored (2012) the dark greens of the algae and vegitation give this area a grimy and dark feel. The unnatural blue glow of the whale oil in the distance leads the players eye to one of the objectives of the level.
